FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to things homeowners ask us all the time.

Do you offer same-day service?

Yes — call us before noon on weekdays and we'll do our best to schedule same-day. Availability varies by job type and current schedule.

Are your estimates really free?

Completely free and no-obligation. We assess your job, give you a transparent quote, and you decide — no pressure, no follow-up spam.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. All ProFix technicians are fully licensed, bonded, and carry general liability insurance. We're happy to provide proof before any job starts.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

We back every job with our satisfaction guarantee. If something isn't right, contact us and we'll return to make it right — at no additional charge.

Do you do work outside your service area?

For larger projects we may travel further — just ask. We serve all of Riverside County and some areas of San Bernardino County.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, check, Venmo, Zelle, and all major credit cards. Payment is due upon job completion unless otherwise arranged.
